# 🛒 Sales Forecasting & Business Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing supermarket sales data to uncover valuable business insights and forecast future sales using Time Series Analysis. The project combines data visualization, business intelligence, and predictive analytics to help stakeholders make informed business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Objective

To contribute to business success by utilizing data analysis techniques, specifically **Time Series Analysis**, to provide valuable insights and accurate **Sales Forecasting**.

---

## 🚀 Project Goals

- Build an interactive business dashboard.
- Analyze sales performance and identify key business trends.
- Forecast future sales using Time Series Analysis.
- Generate actionable insights to support strategic decision-making.

---

## 📊 Features

### 1. Dashboard Creation
- Designed an interactive dashboard.
- Identified and tracked important KPIs.
- Added dynamic filters and slicers.
- Created visually appealing charts and graphs.
- Enabled users to explore data at different levels.

### 2. Data Analysis
- Performed exploratory data analysis (EDA).
- Analyzed sales trends and business performance.
- Visualized important metrics using charts and dashboards.
- Identified patterns and business opportunities.

### 3. Sales Forecasting
- Applied Time Series Analysis.
- Used historical sales data to predict future sales.
- Generated sales forecasts for the next **15 days**.
- Improved business planning through predictive analytics.

### 4. Business Recommendations
- Provided actionable business insights.
- Supported strategic decision-making.
- Identified opportunities for growth and operational efficiency.
